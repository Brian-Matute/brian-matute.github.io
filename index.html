About Me

My name is Brian Matute, and I’m a sophomore majoring in broadcast journalism. I’ve been a Miami Hurricanes fan for as long as I can remember. I was born and raised in Miami and have always had a love and passion for sports and broadcasting.

I’ve always had a love for learning about new and interesting stories. I love wrapping my head around the intricacies that come in each story. For this reason, I love watching shows and movies and discussing them. However, my love for stories doesn’t stop at movies and shows, as I also have a love for stories as they relate to sports.

As I continue my tenure here at the University of Miami, I have some goals set in mind. I hope to foster new connections and grow my networking skills.
